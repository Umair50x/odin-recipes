Ribeye Steak Recipe

Description

Great Japanese teriyaki-style marinated ribeye steak with a from-scratch teriyaki sauce.

With a prep time of 10 minutes and cook time of 15 with additional 2 hrs for a serving of 2.

Per Serving: 297 calories; protein 19.6g; carbohydrates 13.5g; fat 18.1g; cholesterol 60.3mg; sodium 991.6mg.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons water2 tablespoons water
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons honey
  • 1 ½ teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 ¼ teaspoons distilled white vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • ¼ teaspoon onion powder
  • ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground ginger
  • 2 (6 ounce) lean beef rib eye steaks

    • Steps:

    1. Whisk together the soy sauce, water, sugar, honey, Worcestershire sauce, vinegar, olive oil, onion powder, garlic powder, and ground ginger in a large bowl. Pierce steaks several times with a fork. Marinate steaks in soy sauce mixture for at least 2 hours.
    2. Whisk together the soy sauce, water, sugar, honey, Worcestershire sauce, vinegar, olive oil, onion powder, garlic powder, and ground ginger in a large bowl. Pierce steaks several times with a fork. Marinate steaks in soy sauce mixture for at least 2 hours.
    3. Serve and Enjoy!
